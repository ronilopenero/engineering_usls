Club Fair

Posted Jan 7, 2014 by Jherson Colonia

UNIVERSITY OF ST. LA SALLE - the College of Engineering and Technology together with the other colleges joined the Club Fair 2013 with the theme “The Realm: Land of the Great” held at the USLS Coliseum, July 24-25.

The Club Fair is an annual event of the University wherein different clubs and organizations will showcase their different programs for your entertainment and learning through the educational club booths.This year, clubs were divided into four groups basing on the four elements- Water, Air, Fire and Earth.

All six engineering clubs joined the club fair; the Engineering Society (ES), Junior Philippine Institute of Chemical Engineers (JPICHE), Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines (IECEP), Computer Hardware Interfacing and Programming Society (CHIPS), Philippine Instrumentation and Controls Society (PICS) and Materials Engineering Student Society (MESS).

During the club fair there were games and activities namely The Mob, Pitch Perfect, Ultimate La Sallian Game Show and the Club Icons.

JPICHE and ES clubs together with Marketer’s Union, SYMA, USSR, Catalyst and Premed Society that composed the Team Ehecatl had defeated and overcome the obstacles that the other competing club teams had presented in the Pitch Perfect Contest.

“As one of the Pitch Perfect winners, I could honestly say the practices and the event itself have proved to be worth it. We had a few difficulties and an example would be that we were few in number and we couldn’t possibly cover all the components of the song such as the percussion and all the second voices. It was hard at first to adjust to the different vocal ranges of my team mates but we pushed through. Not only did I gain new friends, but also new insights.” Alexa Agabon quoted, one of the members of the team Ehecatl.

The Club Fair ended with the Club Icons, there were around 40 club icons that competed for the title but the MassComm Society bagged the title.

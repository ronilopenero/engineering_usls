Lasallians Venture in Las Islas Piratas

Posted Jan 6, 2013 by Admin

GRANADA ECOPARK - The College of Engineering and Technology, together with the four other colleges, unleashed their Lasallian spirits as they come together in the Summerfest 2013 with the theme: “Las Islas Piratas: Uncover your Lasallian Treasures” on April 30 at Granada Eco Park.

The event is held annually for the benefit of the Lasallian students taking up summer classes to get a day off from the lessons and quizzes in the classroom and to en joy summer vacation

Various activities were heldwhich aimed to develop the camaraderie among students and most especially to live out the Lasallian values in every activity held by the Lasallian community.

Summerfest 2013is a great venue not only for the Engineering and Technology students and faculty but also for the other colleges to get together, unite and reward ourselves for studying hard during the summer class,” said CET Governor Ryan Jake Pisuena.

The College of Arts and Sciences was declared the overall Champion as they bagged the award for Water Volleyball, Body Painting and the Female Summer Icon 2013.

Other winners were the College of Engineering and Technology for the Governor’s Challenge, Male Summer Icon 2013 and the College of Education for the Amazing Race, College Headquarters Design and the Production Showdown.

The College of Engineering and Technology placed 2nd runner-up for over-all performance during Summerfest 2013.
